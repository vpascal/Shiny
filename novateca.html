Novateca is a five-year national program,  providing Moldovan citizens with access to relevant information and modern, locally tailored services in public libraries to enrich their lives, drawing on technology tools and trained librarians who can guide them in this process. Novateca started as a two years pilot grant, equipping 68 libraries with a total of 304 computers across 12 different raions (districts) and trained 243 librarians on ICT skills and new services delivery.
